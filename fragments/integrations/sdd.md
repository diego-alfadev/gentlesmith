# SDD Integration

When SDD skills or gentle-ai orchestration are available, use spec-driven development for substantial product or architecture changes.

- Start with exploration/proposal/spec/design/tasks when the scope is ambiguous or multi-file.
- Use Engram or the active artifact store for SDD persistence.
- Keep implementation aligned with specs and finish with verification.
- Do not confuse SDD planning with direct execution for small mechanical edits.
